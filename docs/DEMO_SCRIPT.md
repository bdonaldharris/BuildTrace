# BuildTrace — Demo Script

**Target length:** 2 minutes
**Format:** Live demo or screen recording
**Audience:** Hackathon judges

---

## Opening (15 seconds)

> "AI helps us build faster than ever. But there's a cost no one talks about — the work gets scattered."

> "Prompts live in chat windows. Errors live in terminals. Decisions live in your head. Commits live in git. And when the session ends, the story of what you actually built — the why, the how, the what came next — is gone."

> "We call that comprehension debt."

---

## The Problem (20 seconds)

> "For a lot of builders — especially Black builders who are building in the margins, building nights and weekends, building with fewer resources and less visibility — that scattered work doesn't just feel messy. It means real skill goes unrecognized. Real innovation goes undocumented. Real work doesn't make it to a portfolio, a LinkedIn post, or a pitch deck."

> "BuildTrace fixes that."

---

## The Demo (60 seconds)

**[Open the app]**

> "This is BuildTrace. You get here with one URL. No login, no setup."

**[Point to the input panel]**

> "Drop in your raw build notes — messy prompts, terminal errors, scattered decisions, whatever you have. Or load the sample session."

**[Click Load Sample Session]**

> "Let's use the sample. This is what a real AI-assisted build session looks like: chaotic, nonlinear, and full of real decisions."

**[Click Generate Recap]**

> "One click."

**[Results appear]**

> "Here's your build timeline — labeled events, in order: prompts, decisions, blockers, fixes, commits, outcomes."

**[Scroll to insight panel]**

> "Here are the decisions you made. Here are the blockers you hit. Here are your next steps."

**[Scroll to Publish Your Build]**

> "And here's where it gets interesting."

**[Click LinkedIn tab]**

> "BuildTrace writes your LinkedIn post for you. Based on what you actually built."

**[Click Blog tab]**

> "Your blog draft. Ready to paste into Notion or Medium."

**[Click copy on one tab]**

> "One click. Copied."

---

## The Impact Close (25 seconds)

> "BuildTrace turns hidden technical work into visible proof of skill, learning, innovation, and ownership."

> "Every builder has built something worth sharing. Most of them just don't have time to document it. We fix that in one paste and one click."

> "This is a 24-hour hackathon POC — but the problem it solves is real, the audience is real, and the path forward is clear."

> "This is BuildTrace."

---

## Notes for Delivery

- Speak slowly — judges are reading the screen and listening at the same time
- Let the UI breathe — pause after clicking Generate so results have time to appear
- Don't apologize for hackathon rough edges — own what you built
- The impact line is the most important sentence — land it clearly
- Have a backup screen recording ready if live demo has connectivity issues
